What does the review page show?

Affects whether you need filter tabs and how much the page grows over time.

A

Pending-only queue

Page shows only candidates with status = pending. Once you act on something it disappears. Simple, zero noise — like an inbox.

B

All candidates with status tabs

Tabs: Pending / Approved / Rejected. You can revisit past decisions and undo them. More useful once there's a lot of history.
